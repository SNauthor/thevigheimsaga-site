The Work

Simon Nilsson writes dark fantasy with a hard edge — stories shaped by violence, consequence, loyalty, and the cost of becoming stronger. The world of The Vigheim Saga draws heavily from Norse atmosphere, brutal survival, and myth-soaked conflict.
